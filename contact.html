The Man Behind It All



Roberto "Bobby" Sulit is the founder and the owner of the business "Eats So Sulit". He is 54 years old and his passion is cooking especially for his loved ones. It all started when he quit his old job after realizing his passion for cooking wherein he looked for a job that involves cooking and started working in a canteen. After some time, he decided to start his own business that involves food delivery that also provides catering services and since he graduated under the course of BS Management, he was able to easily start a business since he had prior knowledge during his college days. After a few years of dedication to cooking, his business has now improved greatly compared to when he started and continues to become more known moving forward.In the present, he takes orders from many people and deliver them via the Lalamove app and although the catering services are not available due to the pandemic, many trust his business and still order from him especially his neighbors and people from his barangay.
